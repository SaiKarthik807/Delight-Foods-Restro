Journey Delight Foods

Delight Foods is a chain of restaurants known for its authentic Indian cuisine. With over 40 years of experience and outlets in Rajahmundry, Hyderabad and Visakhapatnam, Delight Foods comforts is spreading its wings in giving customers a true Andhra dining experience.

Delight Foods has its origins from a small vegetarian hotel in Amalapuram. Mr PeddiRaju , the managing director of Delight Foods was a visionary and at the age 16 he moved from village to village in search of business opportunities. In 1975 he opened the Panduranga Vegetarian hotel in Amalapuram. The Hotel garnered a lot of attention in the surrounding villages and soon proved to be a successful venture.The challenge however gets better as they hit the shores of Visakhapatnam with their new Veg and Nonveg outlet in Siripuram equipped with banquet hall.

In the year 2024,Delight Foods re-branded itself as Delight Foods and opened a new property in Rajamundry. With vibrant interiors bigger kitchen, larger seating capacity and iconic dishes there was hardly any competition for this restaurant. This was followed up with a new restaurant in Somajiguda, Hyderabad in 2014.

In a view to expand the business, the first Delight Foods hotel was established in 1997, Rajahmundry. With a modest two storied building, Delight Foods served both Veg and NonVeg dishes and was also equipped with a mini banquet hall. The Rajamundry outlet proved to be the turning point of this group as they followed it up with a take away branch in Hyderabad in the early 2000.
